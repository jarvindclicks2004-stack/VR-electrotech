Company overview

About VR Electrotech

VR Electrotech is a service‑driven company providing solar and biomedical equipment solutions for homeowners, hospitals, clinics and commercial buildings.

The focus is on practical execution, safety and long‑term support. Every project is approached with clear communication, transparent pricing and attention to detail on site conditions and equipment performance.

Our mission

Reliable power & uptime

VR Electrotech aims to help clients reduce downtime, control energy costs and improve the reliability of critical systems.

By combining solar expertise with biomedical equipment service, VR Electrotech supports facilities where both energy and equipment uptime matter—ranging from individual homes to high‑demand healthcare environments.

  • Solutions tailored for homeowners, hospitals, clinics and commercial sites.
